# 🇲🇼 LendIt Malawi

**A peer-to-peer asset lending platform built for Malawi.**  
Borrow what you need. Earn from what you own.

---

## What is LendIt Malawi?

LendIt Malawi is a community lending app that allows people across Malawi to lend and borrow everyday items — like lawn mowers, generators, tents, tools, and more — at affordable daily rates.

Instead of buying something you only need once, you can borrow it from a neighbour. Instead of letting your items sit unused, you can earn money by listing them.

---

## Features

- 🔍 **Browse & Search** — Find items by category, name, or location
- 📍 **GPS Location** — See listings nearest to you first
- 📅 **Availability Calendar** — Book specific dates, see what's already taken
- 💳 **Secure Payments** — Payments go through the app (Airtel Money / TNM Mpamba). Lender's contact is only revealed after payment — no bypassing possible
- 🔐 **User Accounts** — Register and login with your phone number
- 🔔 **Notifications** — Get alerted when someone books your item
- ⭐ **Reviews** — Rate and review items after borrowing
- 💬 **WhatsApp Integration** — Contact lenders directly after booking
- 🌐 **Bilingual** — Full English and Chichewa support
- ⚙️ **Admin Dashboard** — Manage listings, view transactions, track platform fees

---

## How It Works

1. **Create a free account** with your phone number
2. **List your item** with a daily price and damage deposit
3. **Borrowers find your item** and pay through the app
4. **You receive 90%** of the rental fee — LendIt keeps 10%
5. **Contacts are revealed** after payment so you can arrange pickup
